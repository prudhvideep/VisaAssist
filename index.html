About Us

International Wings is an online education forum that specializes in helping students pursue their dreams of studying abroad. We have a team of experienced and qualified people who can guide you through every step of the visa process, from university shortlisting to visa application and interview preparation. Whether you want to study at a top-ranked university, a community college, or an English language school, we can help you find the best fit for your goals and budget. We also provide post-arrival support and assistance to ensure a smooth transition to your new academic and cultural environment.
